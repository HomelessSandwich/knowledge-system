An adverb is a word that modifies a [[Verb|verb]] (*he sings* ***loudly***), an [[Adjective|adjective]] (***very*** *tall*), another [[Adverb|adverb]] or even a whole sentence (***fortunately***, *I had brought an umbrella*).
